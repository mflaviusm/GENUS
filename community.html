Our Community

At GENUS, gaming is paramount. We welcome everyone in our community and aim to deliver the supreme experience when gaming. We have various channels for our users to interact and game together. We have a dedicated Discord server, a Reddit community page to discuss various topics and ask for advice. We also have a YouTube channel where we post montages from our gaming sessions, including but not limited to fails, 200 IQ moves and various guides and tutorials for different games. You can also find us live streaming almost daily on our Twitch channel.
